Frequently Asked Questions

What is the event about?

This event is a two-day idea pitching competition where participants present innovative solutions in domains like AI, Automation, Business Models, Software Development, and Open Innovation. The event aims to encourage creative thinking and problem-solving through a structured competition with expert judging.

Who can participate in the event?

The event is open to students and professionals from the fields of Computer Science, IT, and related domains. Teams must consist of 2 to 5 members.

Can I participate individually?

No, individual participation is not allowed. All participants must form teams of 2 to 5 members.

What are the domains for the idea pitching?

Participants must choose one of the following domains: AI & Machine Learning Automation & Robotics Business Models & Innovations Software Development & Engineering Open Innovation (cross-domain creative solutions)

What happens in the final round?

The finalists from each domain will be given an on-the-spot problem statement. They will have 1 hour to create a solution and present it to the jury. The winning teams will be awarded based on creativity, technical knowledge, feasibility, and presentation skills.

What are the prizes for winners?

The prizes include trophies for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners. Additionally, special recognition will be given to other notable participants. Certificates will be provided to all participants.

How do I register my team?

Teams can register via the event’s website. Each team must choose a domain and provide details about their team members during the registration process. Keep in mind that registration is limited and must be completed by the given deadline.

Can I change my team members after registering?

may be, once a team is registered, the composition cannot be changed.

What are the rules of the event?

Participants are expected to follow the event’s rules, which include maintaining respect and professionalism, avoiding plagiarism, and adhering to fair play. Any form of cheating, harassment, or inappropriate behavior will lead to disqualification

What will the final round involve?

The final round will involve an on-the-spot problem statement where the 5 domain finalists will have 1 hour to come up with a solution and present it within 10 minutes, followed by a Q&A session with the jury.

Is there a fee to participate?

Yes, there is a registration fee per person ruppes 250/-

If we selected on day 1 we shoud stay any accomdation will provied?

No accomdation provided, the winners of day 1 must come to day 2 final round
